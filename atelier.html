AYA MUSE WORLD

Boutique Digital Atelier

Not an agency. A boutique digital atelier — for founders and lifestyle brands that need a visual world before the world knows their name.

We create boutique websites, brandbooks and visual identities for emerging luxury, hospitality, beauty and lifestyle brands — from fragrance houses and wellness studios to hotels, restaurants and founder-led labels.

Each project begins with one question: how should this brand feel before anyone buys, books or follows?

From editorial landing pages and mobile app concepts to brandbooks and Pinterest-first campaigns, we build digital presence with structure, taste and a clear reason to return.

BRANDBOOK PORTFOLIO

Yacht / Private Charter / Visual Identity

AYA — Yacht Brandbook

A 20-page portfolio brandbook for AYA — a private Mediterranean charter concept built around the line “the art of leaving noise behind”. The book covers brand essence, audience, tone of voice, values, editorial direction and the kind of restraint that suits guests who have already been admired and now wish to be unbothered.

Portfolio example. Shown to demonstrate the quiet-luxury end of our range — ultra-private, restrained, and written for clients who prefer to disappear beautifully. The atelier also works with more expressive luxury and lifestyle brands.

Fragrance / Product Identity / Editorial Direction

AYA Parfum — Blue Orchid

A 20-page fragrance brandbook for AYA Parfum, Blue Orchid Chapter — a niche perfumery concept built around the line “the art of becoming a memory”. Covers bottle, label, box, discovery set, paper details, photography direction, audience and a deep blue-orchid mood.

Portfolio example. Made to show how a fragrance idea becomes a complete product world — from name to packaging to campaign — for the kind of brand that earns shelf space at a niche perfumery rather than mass retail.
