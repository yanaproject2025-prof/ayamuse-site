Aya — a space for emotional support, self-reflection, and digital care

1. General Information

Aya is a mobile application for emotional support, self-reflection, journaling, and personal digital care.

This Privacy Policy & Terms of Use (“Policy”) applies to the Aya mobile application, the Aya Muse World website, and related digital services operated under the Aya brand.

By accessing or using Aya, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Policy.

2. Important Disclaimer

Aya is not a medical, psychological, psychiatric, therapeutic, or crisis service.

Aya does not provide medical advice, mental health diagnosis, therapy, treatment, or emergency assistance.

All content, features, reflections, and AI-generated responses inside Aya are intended only for emotional support, self-reflection, inspiration, and personal journaling.

Some responses inside Aya may be generated with the assistance of artificial intelligence. AI-generated responses may not always be accurate, complete, or appropriate for your personal situation.

If you are experiencing a crisis, severe emotional distress, or feel that you may harm yourself or others, please contact qualified professionals, local emergency services, or crisis support resources immediately.

3. Data We May Collect

Aya may collect and process only the data necessary to operate and improve the application, including:

  • email address;
  • authentication data;
  • user-generated notes, letters, journal entries, and reflections;
  • selected emotional states and app interactions;
  • technical device and app data;
  • data required to provide push notifications.

Aya does not sell, rent, or trade personal data for advertising purposes.

4. How We Use Data

We use data only for the following purposes:

  • to operate and improve the application;
  • to personalize the user experience;
  • to save user content and app progress;
  • to maintain security, reliability, and stability;
  • to provide AI-powered features;
  • to provide notifications if enabled by the user.

5. AI Features and Third-Party Services

Some Aya features may use artificial intelligence and trusted cloud infrastructure provided by third-party services, including OpenAI and Google Firebase.

When you use AI-powered features, certain user messages or inputs may be processed by these services solely to generate responses and support the functionality of the application.

Third-party services may process data according to their own privacy and security practices. Aya uses such services only as necessary to provide core app functionality.

6. Data Storage, Security, and Encryption

Aya uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data.

Some app features may use local encryption and secure storage on the user’s device.

Certain data may be stored using secure cloud infrastructure such as Google Firebase.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. However, we work to protect your data using appropriate safeguards.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide the application’s features, maintain user accounts, comply with legal obligations, and ensure service security.

Users may request deletion of their account and related data through the application settings or by contacting us.

After deletion, some limited data may be retained only where required by law, security, fraud prevention, or legitimate operational purposes.

8. User Responsibilities

By using Aya, you agree to:

  • use the application respectfully and lawfully;
  • not misuse, disrupt, or attempt to harm the service;
  • not upload illegal, harmful, abusive, or offensive content;
  • understand the symbolic, reflective, and non-clinical nature of Aya;
  • remain responsible for your personal decisions and actions.

10. Children and Age Restrictions

Aya is not intended for children under the age required by applicable local law to use digital services without parental consent.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete such data.

Aya may be used by people from different countries and regions. By using the application, you understand that your data may be processed and stored in jurisdictions outside your country of residence.
