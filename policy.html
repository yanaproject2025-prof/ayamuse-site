Privacy & Terms

A shared policy for our website, application & digital spaces

Privacy Policy

Aya Muse World respects your privacy. We do not collect, sell, or use personal data for tracking, profiling, advertising, or behavioral analysis.

Our website, application, and digital platforms are designed to be accessed without creating accounts or submitting personal information.

For security, performance, and content delivery purposes, we may rely on trusted infrastructure services such as Cloudflare. These services operate solely as a technical and security layer and do not engage in user analytics, profiling, or advertising.

Aya Muse World does not use cookies or similar technologies for marketing or analytics purposes.

Terms of Use

Aya Muse World is an aesthetic, symbolic, and editorial project. All content is provided for inspiration, emotional reflection, and gentle support only.

Aya is not a licensed therapist, psychologist, medical professional, or crisis service. The project does not provide medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice.

If you are experiencing serious distress or require professional help, please seek assistance from qualified specialists or local emergency services.

By using this website, application, or related digital content, you acknowledge full responsibility for your personal decisions and well-being.

The Symbolic Nature of Aya

Aya is a symbolic and poetic presence — a visual and narrative voice of care, softness, and reflection.

Aya does not replace real human connection, professional support, or real-world care systems.

Global Scope & Respect

Aya Muse World is an international project created for people from different cultures, countries, languages, and backgrounds.

The project promotes inclusivity, respect, and emotional safety and does not tolerate discrimination, harm, or judgment in any form.
